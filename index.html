To apply for a hire purchase car from us, you'll need to meet our eligibility criteria, and we continue to review it on an ongoing basis to keep up with industry best practices.

Our basic eligibility requirements are listed below, however our compliance team may request for more information during the application process.

  • Drivers must have a clean and no criminal record.
  • Drivers must be over 25 years with minimum a of 5 years
    driving experience.
  • Drivers must be able to read and write in English language.
  • Drivers must have a valid driver’s license and have a good
    knowledge of Abuja terrain.
  • Drivers must be able to make a security deposit.
  • Drivers must have an eligible guarantor with a clean and no
    criminal record.

About Us

Commercial drivers borrow cars for a certain fee. It’s an established system that works but doesn’t
necessarily offer good value to drivers.

We believe commercial drivers should be working harder for themselves and not for someone else, so we founded Rapyd on that principle. We created a platform and began helping people who need cars for commercial purposes, providing better value to them. It’s as simple as that.

We’re not a replacement for banks that give car loans, and we don’t pretend to offer the lounge service or marble foyer that may be experienced elsewhere. We exist for commercial drivers who would like to own the vehicle once all necessary payments have been made.

At the start of the agreement a deposit will be paid. The higher the initial deposit the lower the monthly payments will be. The fixed regular monthly payment and the term length will then be agreed depending on the driver’s circumstances and preferences. With no deferred lump sum to pay at the end, the driver will become the legal owner of the vehicle once all the monthly payments have been paid.
